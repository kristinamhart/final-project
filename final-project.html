The Rising Cost of Fruits and Vegtables

Exploring how fruit and vegetable prices have changed over time.

Why Food Prices Matter

Grocery prices have become a growing concern across the United States. Fruits and vegetables, which are essential to a healthy diet, have increased in price over the years. For many families, these rising costs affect shopping habits, meal planning, and access to nutritious food.

Comparing Fruit Prices

This visualization compares the prices of different fruits. Some fruits remain relatively affordable, while others have become significantly more expensive due to transportation costs, weather conditions, and supply chain disruptions.

“Healthy eating becomes more difficult when nutritious foods become luxury items.”

Vegetable Prices Over Time

Vegetable prices have also increased, particularly for produce affected by droughts, inflation, and rising transportation expenses. This chart highlights trends in the costs of commonly purchased vegetables.

Comparing Food Costs

This comparison visualization highlights differences in pricing between fruits and vegetables. While some foods remain relatively affordable, others have increased dramatically due to inflation, transportation costs, and supply shortages.

Conclusion

Rising food costs are more than just economic statistics they directly impact nutrition, household budgets, and public health. By visualizing these price changes, we can better understand how inflation and supply chain issues shape everyday life.

Data storytelling helps transform numbers into meaningful narratives. Instead of simply listing prices, these visualizations show how trends connect to broader social and economic issues.
